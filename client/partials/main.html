How do we support large rooftop project financing?

We are working with private investors looking for credible roof-owners to fund large projects on a pay-per-unit basis. We can arrange financing for projects with the following specifications:
  • 80KW + project size
  • All-states are acceptable
  • Net metering desirable, not mandatory
  • The customer should be willing to share their financial details for due-diligence
  • Typical contract duration 10-20 years
  • BOOT price starting at Rs.5/KWh

How do we get the Monitoring App?

Each project financed by Oorjan, comes with our proprietary Monitoring App that provides information to the customer and Oorjan information about the project performance during the loan term. To the customer, it will also give Alerts and Notifications for cleaning and any any other maintenance requirement. In case of a problem, the customer can contact us for support.

You can download the App from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store and check out a live system at Bangalore.

How to understand if the proposal that the customer has received is correct?

As part of the financing process, Oorjan ensures if the system components are MNRE qualified and also meet all other State mandated criteria. Systems received from Oorjan Installer Partners typically satisfy this criterion. For systems by other installers, as part of the due-diligence, Oorjan will provide any necessary feedback.

Does Oorjan finance MW scale projects?

We finance any rooftop and captive projects.
Unfortunately, we cannot help with open access / large scale MW projects.

Can we provide working capital financing to the Installers?

Our current financiers are only financing end customers. So we are not currently enabling working capital financing to the service providers.

Do we support off-grid systems or systems with batteries?

Yes. Large part of the country does not have stable electricity and one of the key drivers for considering solar is that it can be used to charge batteries. Oorjan will follow the same quality verification process for battery based systems, but enable funding for them as well.

Can we arrange funds for social causes or donations?

We are happy to seek donors when the system is required for BPL families, social institutes, etc. The availability of funds depends on case-by-case basis. Please contact us with the details of who the funds are being sought out for.

I am an entrepreneur and have just started my solar business in my home town. Can Oorjan support me?

Absolutely yes!
We want more and more service providers and the system installers to be able to serve customers in their locality. There are many roofs in the country and we need a lot of trained personnel to work on them. If you need help with system design, procurement or financing, we will support your start-up.

If I am an investment firm OR a private investor, can Oorjan help us finance solar projects?

Absolutely yes!
Connecting investors to the projects is the primary motivation behind Oorjan’s financing platform. We will work with you on your financial expectations and identify projects that meet that goal. Please contact us to discuss further.
